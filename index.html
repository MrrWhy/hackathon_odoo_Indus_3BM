Gmail-Based Login
Sign in with your Google account. Access is automatically determined by your registered email — no passwords, no hassle.
✓ OAuth simulated

Administrator Panel
A dedicated Admin role can add users, change roles (Admin/Manager/Warehouse), deactivate accounts, and control every access privilege.
✓ Live in app

Stock Management
Add, edit, and monitor stock levels. Low-stock alerts and reorder level tracking keep you ahead of shortages automatically.
✓ Live in app

Delivery Dashboard

✓ Live in app
